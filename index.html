How to Play

🔤A hidden phrase is shown as blank tiles. Guess one letter at a time using the keyboard.
🟩Green tiles — the letter appears in the phrase and is revealed in every position.
⬛Dark keys — that letter is not in the phrase. You lose one life.
❤️You have 6 lives. Too many wrong guesses and it's game over.
💡The category is shown as a hint. A new phrase every day!
